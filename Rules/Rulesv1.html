The Murder Mystery Rules
________________________________

  • Every three seconds, 6 people will be shown on screen

  • 5 will be innocent and 1 will be a murderer

  • If you click on a innocent, you will lose 2 points

  • If you click on a murderer, you will earn a point

  • This in total will happen 6 times

  • The aim of the game is to spot all 6 murderers and get all 6 points
