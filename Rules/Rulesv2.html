The Murder Mystery Rules
________________________________

  • Once you press play 6 people will be on the screen for a limited time for you to remember their spot.

  • 5 will be innocent and 1 will be a murderer.

  • If you click on a innocent, you will lose.

  • If you click on a murderer, you will win.

  • The aim of the game is to click the murderer and win the game.
